 - whether the agent effectively uses weapons or tools to combat zombies
 - whether the agent defeats a certain number of zombies

 - e.g. whether the agent recognizes ineffective combat strategies and adjusts them
 - whether the agent avoids or addresses situations that lower its health significantly

**Creative Attempts: any creative attempts exhibited by the agent during doing task**
 - e.g. using terrain or environment creatively to gain an advantage in combat
 - e.g. utilizing a variety of weapons or combat strategies

**Task Completion Efficiency**
 - whether the time taken by the agent to complete the task falls within a reasonable range
 - whether effective combat strategies were employed to minimize unnecessary damage or delays

 - whether the agent selects appropriate weapons and gear for combating zombies
