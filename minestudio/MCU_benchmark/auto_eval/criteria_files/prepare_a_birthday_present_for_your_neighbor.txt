**Task Progress: the key factors/steps for completing the task** 
 - whether the agent gathers necessary materials for the present
 - whether the agent crafts or selects an item as a present
 - whether the agent delivers the present to the neighbor (i.e., placing it in a chest or giving it directly)

**Action Control: whether the agents have unrelated operations of the task, including useless actions and redundancy actions**
 - e.g. gathering unrelated materials, crafting unrelated items, wandering aimlessly

**Error Recognition and Correction: whether the agent can promptly identify and rectify its mistakes**
 - e.g. whether the agent recognizes incorrect materials or items and replaces them
 - whether the corrected results demonstrate improvement and reduce flaws in the final product.

**Creative Attempts: any creative attempts exhibited by the agent during doing task**
 - e.g. adding decorative elements to the present, selecting unique or rare items as a present, writing a personalized message on a sign

**Task Completion Efficiency**
 - whether the time taken by the agent to complete the task falls within a reasonable range.
 - whether effective collection and crafting strategies were employed to minimize unnecessary repetitions or errors.

**Material Selection and Usage: whether the agent correctly utilize the given materials**
 - whether the agent selects appropriate and meaningful items for the present
 - whether the agent uses the materials efficiently without excessive waste
