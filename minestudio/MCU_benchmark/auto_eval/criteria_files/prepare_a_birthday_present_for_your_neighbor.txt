**Task Progress: the key factors/steps for completing the task** 
 - whether the agent gathers necessary materials for the present
 - whether the agent crafts a present (e.g., cake, crafted item)
 - whether the agent delivers the present to the neighbor's location

**Action Control: whether the agents have unrelated operations of the task, including useless actions and redundancy actions**
 - e.g. collecting unrelated materials, crafting unrelated items, wandering aimlessly

**Error Recognition and Correction: whether the agent can promptly identify and rectify its mistakes**
 - e.g. whether the agent recognizes incorrect crafting recipes and corrects them
 - whether the agent identifies and replaces inappropriate materials for the present

**Creative Attempts: any creative attempts exhibited by the agent during doing task**
 - e.g. creating a personalized card or message along with the present
 - e.g. designing unique wrapping or packaging for the present

**Task Completion Efficiency**
 - whether the time taken by the agent to complete the task falls within a reasonable range
 - whether effective strategies were employed to minimize unnecessary repetitions or errors

**Material Selection and Usage: whether the agent correctly utilize the given materials** 
 - whether the agent selects appropriate materials for crafting the present
 - whether the agent effectively uses the materials without wastage
