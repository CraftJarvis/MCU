 - whether the agent uses shears to carve the pumpkin

 - e.g. carve multiple pumpkins with different expressions
 - e.g. use carved pumpkins for decoration, like placing them on fences or creating a pumpkin patch

 - whether the time taken by the agent to complete the task falls within a reasonable range.
 - whether effective carving strategies were employed to minimize unnecessary repetitions or errors.
